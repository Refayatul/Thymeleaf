Our Experience

The environment at DSi was very friendly and welcoming. During our tour, we discovered the company has four main units:

  • Service Unit 1
  • Service Unit 2
  • E-Government Unit
  • Product Unit

Some interesting observations from our tour:

  • There was a PlayStation area for gaming and relaxation
  • Well-maintained prayer rooms for employees
  • Most developers were using Ubuntu Linux
  • We also saw machines running MacOS and Windows
  • For the first time, we saw someone using openSUSE Linux
  • Several conferences were happening simultaneously during our tour

Our day started at 11:00 AM and the office closed at 4:00 PM. Despite the short day, we got a comprehensive introduction to the company's culture and facilities.
